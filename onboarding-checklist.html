Business Information
The basics about your company
Required
Legal business name
The exact name as registered — and the name you want displayed publicly if different.
Business address or city
Used for your website, Google Business Profile, and local SEO.
Business phone number
The number you want customers to call. Can be a direct line or a new number we help you set up.
Business email address
Ideally a branded email (you@yourbusiness.com). If you only have Gmail, that's fine for now.
Industry and type of business
e.g. Real estate brokerage, residential HVAC, family law firm, e-commerce clothing brand.
Description of your ideal customer
Who do you want to attract? Be specific — age, location, budget, what problem they have.
Key products or services with pricing
A list of what you offer and how much each costs. e.g. "Basic cleaning $150, Deep clean $280". Approximates are fine.
🎨
Brand & Visual Identity
Files and colors that define how you look
Required
Logo file(s)
Best formats: SVG, PNG with transparent background, or AI/EPS. If you only have a JPEG that's okay — we'll work with what you have.
Brand colors (hex codes or description)
Exact hex codes (#3B82F6) are ideal. If you don't know them, describe in words: "shiny gold like a new coin, deep navy blue."
Headshots or professional photos
At least one high-quality photo of you or your team. Minimum 1MB, ideally on a clean background.
Any brand photos or lifestyle images you own
Before/after photos, photos of your space, product shots, team at work. The more the better — we'll curate.
3 words that describe your brand
e.g. "Bold, Trustworthy, Modern" or "Warm, Expert, Approachable." This shapes the tone of all copy we write.
Brands or websites you visually admire (optional)
Links or names — any industry. Just brands whose look and feel you love. e.g. Apple, Stripe, Peloton, a local competitor.
🌐
Website Content
Copy and information that goes on your site
Required
Short "About" bio or story
A few sentences about you / your company — who you are, how long you've been operating, what drives you. We'll polish it.
Complete list of services and descriptions
Each service/product you want featured on the site — name, brief description, and pricing if public-facing.
Customer testimonials or reviews
Copy/paste from Google, Yelp, Facebook, or email threads. Even 2–3 good ones make a big difference on your site.
Your unique selling point or guarantee
What do you promise that competitors don't? e.g. "48-hour response," "same-day service," "5-year warranty."
FAQs your customers commonly ask (optional)
5–10 questions you hear repeatedly. We'll build a FAQ section and use these to train your AI chatbot.
🔐
Domain & Hosting Access
Login credentials for your web presence
Required
Your domain name (or desired domain)
e.g. yourbusiness.com — if you don't own one yet, we'll help you pick and register one.
Domain registrar login (if transferring)
GoDaddy, Namecheap, Google Domains, etc. We'll need access to update DNS. Share securely — never via email. We'll send a secure link.
Current hosting provider (if applicable)
Wix, Squarespace, GoDaddy, WP Engine, Bluehost — whatever your existing site runs on.

Social Media Handles
Your existing profiles across platforms
Optional

Only check what you have. If you're starting fresh on a platform, leave it unchecked — we'll set it up.

Instagram handle and login
@yourbusiness — we'll need admin access to connect to your scheduler and post on your behalf.
Facebook Business Page login
Required for Meta Ads, page management, and lead form integrations.
Google Business Profile email
The Gmail used to manage your Google Business listing. We'll verify and optimize it for local SEO.
LinkedIn company page URL (optional)
Needed for LinkedIn content scheduling if you're on the Growth or Scale plan.
TikTok or YouTube handle (optional)
Only if you currently have a presence. Not required to get started.

Existing Tools & Software
What you're already using — we'll plug in or replace
Optional

Check any tools you currently use. Don't worry if you use none — that's normal and we'll set everything up for you.

CRM system (GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.)
Name of the system and roughly how many contacts/leads are in it. We'll migrate or integrate.
Email marketing platform (Mailchimp, Klaviyo, Constant Contact, etc.)
Include your current subscriber count if you have one. We'll migrate your list.
Scheduling or booking tool (Calendly, Acuity, Jane, etc.)
We'll connect this to your site, your CRM, and your automated follow-up sequences.
Payment processor (Stripe, Square, PayPal, etc.)
We'll connect payments to your CRM for automatic deal tracking and receipt automation.
Google Ads or Facebook Ads account
Share your account ID or Business Manager access. We'll audit existing campaigns and build from there.
Any other tools you rely on
Quickbooks, Zapier, Slack, project management tools, SMS platforms, review tools — anything you use daily.
